<h1>Interview with the Vampire by Anne Rice
(The Vampire Chronicles: Book 1)</h1>
<div itemprop="mainEntity" itemscope="" itemtype="https://schema.org/Book">
<meta content="Interview with the Vampire" itemprop="name"/>
<meta content="Anne Rice" itemprop="author"/>
<div itemprop="review" itemscope="" itemtype="http://schema.org/Review">
<div itemprop="reviewBody">
<p>In a darkened room a young man sits telling the macabre and eerie story of his life - the story of a vampire, gifted with eternal life, cursed with an exquisite craving for human blood. Anne Rice's compulsively readable novel is arguably the most celebrated work of vampire fiction since Bram Stoker's Dracula was published in 1897. As the Washington Post said on its first publication, it is a 'thrilling, strikingly original work of the imagination ...sometimes horrible, sometimes beautiful, always unforgettable'.</p><p><span class="rating">9/10</span> <em>'One of the most wonderful, erotic, sensual books ever written.</em></p>
